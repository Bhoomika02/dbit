Founder view

Don Bosco ISE Innovation Lab has been established with an aim to provide a platform and mechanism for the internal & External stakeholders to interact with each other and thus to foster needs/opportunities based on innovation.
The importance of education and innovation is undisputed in helping a nation become a global leader and in maintaining its leadership. Taking into the account the diverse needs of people from across India, regional growth becomes the foundation for national development.
The Don Bosco ISE Innovation Lab nurtures innovation and encourages entrepreneurial talents among students, faculty and people of the region.
The Innovation Lab seeks to build an innovation network with multiple stakeholders like Industry, other institutions, R&D Labs and others. This Lab will act as the focal point and will be able to leverage:

  • The technology R&D and problem solving strengths of the DBISEIL
  • The entrepreneurial spirit of the students and faculty
  • Collaboration with local industry, NGOs and others
  • The teaching and training capabilities of the DBISEIL
  • Government policy initiatives more efficiently

Inovation club

Innovation Lab - 2020 is initiated by the Department of Information Science & Engineering, under the leadership of Dr. Gowramma, Head of the Department, ISE, DBIT. The main motive of the lab is to encourage students to gain knowledge in various technological fields, acquire the required skill sets to face the competitive world and improve themselves to become industry ready. The introductory session for the Lab was held on the 8th of November, 2020. The session was hosted by Dr. Gowramma and addressed by Dr. Gowramma and Mr. Swaraj, Owner of ‘School of IoT’, Student, DBIT. Topics discussed in the introductory session were as follows:

  • Introduction to the Innovation Lab and its main objective.
  • Approach towards the Lab and its events.
  • Encouragement to the students to be a part of the initiative.
  • A glimpse of the conduction of several activities.
  • Announcement of the courses, teams and the team leaders.
The introductory session brought all the students together to know the reason of the initiation of the Lab and the conduction of various activities. It was a very interactive session in which the students came up with their own ideas and suggestions in addition to the proposed ones. Few students initiated themselves to serve as team leaders to different groups. The students straight forwardly raised their comments on the topics which were not understandable by them and cleared their ambiguity with the host.
